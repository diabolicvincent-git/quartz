### Fußwurzel / Tarsus
- **Proximaler Tarsus** (proximal → distal)
	- *Talus / Sprungbein*
	- *Calcaneus / Fersenbein*
	- *Os naviculare / Kahnbein*
- **Distaler Tarsus** 
	- *Os cuneiforme mediale, intermedium, laterale / Keilbeine*
	- *Os cuboideum / Würfelbein*
